About Gausin

India's Trusted Name in Process Engineering

Gausin International Engineers Pvt. Ltd. is a leading industrial engineering company headquartered in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh India. We specialize in the design, engineering, fabrication, and supply of technologically advanced industrial process equipment.

Our portfolio spans evaporators, dryers, milk chilling centres, CIP systems, heat exchangers, pressure vessels, waste management, ZLD, and complete turnkey process plants - serving dairy, pharmaceutical, food & Beverage, chemical, distillery, pulp & paper, and energy industries across India and internationally.
