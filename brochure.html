Company Overview

Engineering that moves from concept to plant floor.

Gausin International Engineers Pvt. Ltd. is an industrial engineering and equipment company headquartered in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, India. The company designs, fabricates and supplies process equipment and turnkey plant systems to manufacturers across dairy, food, pharmaceutical, chemical, and effluent treatment sectors across India.

The working approach is process-first and project-focused: understand the process duty in detail, select the right equipment configuration, engineer around actual operating constraints, fabricate under a documented quality plan, and support the client from installation through to long-term operational service.

This integrated capability — covering process study, engineering, fabrication, automation readiness, site commissioning and after-sales — makes Gausin a single point of accountability for technically demanding projects where multiple engineering disciplines must work together.

Engineering Capabilities

A complete capability for process equipment projects.

Every plant has its own product characteristics, energy target, layout constraint and maintenance requirement. Our project work is structured around these realities — from the first process discussion through to equipment handover and long-term operational support.

Process Study & Equipment Selection

Evaluation of feed properties, concentration or drying duty, thermal sensitivity, fouling tendency, utility availability and capacity objective to determine the correct equipment configuration before engineering begins.

Thermal & Mechanical Engineering

Heat-transfer area sizing, pressure-part design to ASME Section VIII / IS 2825 / IBR, material selection, GA drawing preparation, P&ID development and utility load estimation for each project.

Fabrication Under Quality Plan

SS 304 / SS 316L equipment fabricated under a documented QAP covering material verification, in-process weld inspection, dimensional checks, surface-finish confirmation, hydrostatic testing and documentation before dispatch.

PLC / SCADA Automation Readiness

Instrumentation provision, control philosophy, safety interlock design and HMI configuration aligned to production targets, regulatory requirements and operator skill levels at the client's site.

Site Installation & Commissioning

Installation supervision, utility connection verification, trial runs, performance checks against agreed parameters, operator orientation and formal handover for production service.

After-Sales Service

Spare parts supply, preventive maintenance schedules, remote troubleshooting support and equipment upgrade recommendations to sustain performance throughout the operational life of each system supplied.

Project Execution

A disciplined path from enquiry to commissioned plant.

The project workflow is structured to reduce technical ambiguity, keep engineering decisions visible to the client and ensure the delivered system is fully aligned with the operating duty expected at site — before fabrication begins and throughout commissioning.

1
Define

Process data, product characteristics, utility availability, site layout, capacity targets and quality requirements captured in detail before any engineering work begins.

2
Engineer

Equipment sizing, heat-transfer calculations, material selection, GA drawings, P&IDs, control philosophy, utility load estimate and client review before fabrication approval.

3
Manufacture

Fabrication under QAP: incoming material check, in-process weld inspection, dimensional verification, hydrostatic test and surface-finish confirmation before dispatch.

4
Commission

Installation support, utility connection verification, trial runs, performance checks against agreed parameters, operator training and formal handover for production use.

Engineering standards followed

Pressure vessels to ASME Section VIII / IS 2825 / IBR. Heat exchangers to TEMA. Dairy and hygienic equipment to 3-A Sanitary Standards and FSSAI norms. Electrical and instrumentation to IS / IEC standards. Full documentation supplied with every project: GA drawings, P&IDs, material test reports, weld records, hydrostatic test certificates and O&M manuals.
