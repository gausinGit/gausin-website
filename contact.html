FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products, services, and project delivery process.

What types of evaporators do you manufacture?

We manufacture Falling Film Evaporators, Forced Circulation Evaporators, Rising Film Evaporators, Plate Type Evaporators, and Batch Vacuum Evaporators. All types are available in single, double, triple, and multi-effect configurations with optional TVR (Thermo Vapour Recompressor) / MVR (Mechanical Vapour Recompression) integration.

What is the typical lead time for equipment delivery?

Lead times depend on equipment complexity and size. Standard equipment typically takes 8 - 12 weeks from order confirmation. Complete turnkey plants or complex multi-effect systems may require 16 - 24 weeks. We provide a detailed project schedule at the time of order placement.

Do you provide installation and commissioning services?

Yes, we provide complete installation supervision and commissioning services as part of our turnkey project offering. Our field engineers manage site installation, mechanical alignment, piping connections, electrical hook-up, pre-commissioning checks, startup, performance testing, and operator training.

What international standards do your products conform to?

Our equipment is designed and fabricated to conform to ASME Section VIII (pressure vessels), TEMA (heat exchangers), IBR (Indian Boiler Regulations), BIS/IS standards, 3-A and EHEDG (hygienic dairy/food equipment), GMP guidelines (pharmaceutical), and PED (EU Pressure Equipment Directive) where required.

Do you provide after-sales support and spare parts?

Yes, we provide comprehensive after-sales support including: original spare parts supply, on-site maintenance visits, preventive maintenance programs, performance optimization audits, remote technical support, and operator retraining as needed. We maintain a dedicated after-sales service team.

Can you handle complete turnkey dairy plant projects?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience in complete turnkey dairy plant projects - from initial process design, equipment selection, and P&ID development through fabrication, supply, installation, commissioning, performance testing, and operator training. We have delivered plants ranging from 5,000 L/day to 200,000 L/day capacity.
