Our Story

Pioneering Process Engineering in India

Gausin International Engineers Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a singular vision: to bring world-class, technologically advanced, environmentally friendly, and energy-efficient industrial process engineering solutions to Indian and global industries at competitive costs, without compromising on quality or performance.

Headquartered in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, we have grown from a specialized evaporator manufacturer to a full-spectrum industrial engineering company. Our expertise spans the complete lifecycle of process plants - from initial feasibility and process design to detailed engineering, fabrication, supply, installation, commissioning, and after-sales support.

With a team of dedicated process engineers, fabricators, and project managers, we have successfully delivered 100+ projects across eight diverse industrial sectors - dairy, pharmaceutical, food processing, chemical, distillery, paper, textile, and energy.
