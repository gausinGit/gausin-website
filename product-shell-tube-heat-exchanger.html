How It Works

1
Shell & Tube Fluid Entry
Hot process fluid enters tube side; cooling or heating medium flows through the shell side.
2
Turbulent Tube Flow
Fluid passes through multiple U-tube or straight tubes; baffles on shell side improve heat transfer.
3
Heat Exchange
Heat transfers across tube walls; suitable for viscous products, steam heating, or high-pressure duty.
4
Temperature Stabilization
Outlet temperature controlled by utility flow rate; design handles fouling-prone or particulate fluids.
5
Product & Utility Outlet
Processed fluid exits at target temperature; condensate or utility returns to boiler, chiller, or CIP loop.
