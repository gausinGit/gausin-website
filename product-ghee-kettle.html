Key Advantages

Even Steam-Jacket Heating
Full-jacket design with scrape agitator prevents hot spots and burn-on during ghee making.
Clear Ghee Production
Open or sight-glass kettle allows visual monitoring of clarification and moisture evaporation.
Scrape Agitator Action
Teflon or SS scraper blades keep milk solids in motion for uniform heating and faster processing.
Flexible Batch Volumes
Kettle sizes from 50 L to 2,000 L for sweet shops, dairies, and industrial ghee plants.
Temperature Control
Steam pressure regulation and RTD monitoring maintain optimal clarification temperature.
Easy Residue Removal
Tilting or bottom-drain design simplifies beri (milk solid) discharge between batches.

How It Works

1
Fat / Butter Loading
Raw butter or cream loaded into the jacketed ghee kettle to required batch quantity.
2
Melting Under Steam Heat
Steam jacket heats contents; scrape agitator prevents local overheating and burn-on.
3
Foam Breaking & Clarification
Agitation breaks foam; milk solids rise and are skimmed during the heating cycle.
4
Moisture Evaporation
Heating continues until moisture evaporates and ghee becomes clear golden liquid.
5
Ghee Collection
Clear ghee drawn from outlet; residue removed from kettle before next batch.
