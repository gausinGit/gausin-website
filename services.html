Our Process

How We Deliver Projects

A structured, transparent project delivery process that keeps you informed and in control at every phase.

01

Initial Consultation & Requirements

We begin with an in-depth technical discussion to understand your process requirements, product specifications, capacity targets, utilities available, and project constraints.

02

Process Design & Feasibility

Our process engineers develop mass & energy balances, process simulations, and preliminary equipment sizing to validate technical and commercial feasibility.

03

Proposal & Commercial Terms

A detailed technical proposal is submitted - including process description, equipment list, P&ID, technical specifications, delivery schedule, and commercial terms.

04

Detailed Engineering

Upon order confirmation, detailed engineering commences - 3D CAD design, fabrication drawings, P&ID finalization, structural design, and all engineering deliverables.

05

Fabrication & Quality Assurance

Precision fabrication in our workshop using CNC machining, laser cutting, and TIG/MIG welding - with rigorous quality checks, NDT testing, and third-party inspection.

06

Installation & Commissioning

Our field engineering team handles site installation, mechanical alignment, piping, electrical connections, pre-commissioning checks, and plant startup with performance testing.

07

Performance Testing & Handover

Guaranteed performance test run conducted in your presence. All documentation, O&M manuals, PLC programs, and as-built drawings handed over upon project completion.

08

After-Sales Support

Ongoing technical support, preventive maintenance, spare parts supply, and annual performance audits to ensure your plant continues operating at peak efficiency.
