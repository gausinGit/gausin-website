Key Advantages

Uniform Crystal Size
Controlled cooling curve and seeding produce consistent crystal texture in ghee and confectionery.
Programmable Cooling Profile
Jacket temperature and agitation speed set to match product-specific crystallization requirements.
Precise Temperature Control
Glycol or water jacket with RTD feedback maintains setpoint within ±0.5°C during crystal growth.
Gentle Agitation
Slow-speed agitator or scraper prevents crystal breakage while ensuring uniform heat removal.
Multiple Tank Sizes
From 200 L batch units to 5,000 L continuous crystallizers for industrial production.
SS Hygienic Design
SS316 construction with sanitary fittings for food-grade ghee, honey, and syrup applications.

How It Works

1
Hot Concentrate Inlet
Supersaturated syrup or fat concentrate charged into the jacketed crystallization tank.
2
Controlled Cooling
Cooling water or glycol jacket lowers product temperature at a programmed cooling curve.
3
Agitation & Seed Crystal Addition
Slow-speed agitator distributes temperature; seed crystals or scraper blades initiate nucleation.
4
Crystal Growth Phase
Product held at set temperature while crystals grow to desired size and texture.
5
Discharge to Next Stage
Crystallized mass discharged to tempering unit, centrifuge, or packing line as per application.
